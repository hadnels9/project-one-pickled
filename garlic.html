A DIY HOW-TO ON PICKLED GARLIC

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 1/2 CUPS WHITE VINIGER
  • 3 GARLIC CLOVES
  • 1/4 CUP SEA SALT
  • 2-3 HEADS OF DILL
  • 1 TEASPOON CUMIN SEEDS
  • 1 TEASPOON CRUSHED RED PEPPER

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Once all ingrediants have been gathered start by combining the vinegar and salt in a large saucepan. Bring the mixture to a boil; reduce heat and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Begin packing garlic into a sterilized jar leaving 1/2 inch of headspace. Add dill, red pepper and cumin seeds.
  3. Once jar is fully packed with garlic, dill, red pepper and cumin seeds. Use a ladle to pour the vinegar into the jar, leaving 1/2-inch of headspace. Remove air bubbles, clean jar rims, and finish by tightening the lid onto the jar.
  4. Seal jar for a longer shelf life or store in refrigerator for up to 4 months
